Padharo Mhare Desh Rajasthan

Rajasthan 'Land of Kings' is a state in North-western India. It's the largest state of India in terms of area. Rajasthan is a heritage state which was ruled by Rajputs and is home to a number of forts. It has many beautiful cities which give us a mind blowing experience about our culture and artitecture.
The 'Hill of Wisdom' Mount Abu is also situated in the Sirohi district of Rajasthan. Also the 'Pink City' of Jaipur is also situated in this state. It's a very beautiful state for a peaceful vaccation with your family where you can enjoy the Rajasthani cuisine dal-batti-churma.


Let's have a glimpse of what the state of Rajasthan has to offer us!
