UTTAR PRADESH

UttarPradesh is the largest state of India in terms of population and is home to the oldest city known to mankind which is situated at the bank of the holy river Ganges Varanasi.It also has one of the seven wonders of the world which a UNESCO world heritage siteThe Taj Mahal situated in the district of Agara. The world famous Kumbh Mela which held after every 12 years witnesses on of the largest human gathering in world happens in the district of Prayagraj. It is the only state in India where we witness change in language after every 30 to 40 Kilometers. The festival of light Deepawali is one of the major festivity celebrated across the state.In terms of food how can you forget the Jalebi and Samosas.In all it is a diverse state both in terms of culture and language and it's true when we say
Agar UP nhi dekha to India nhi dekha


Let's go on a journey to the wonderful state of Uttar Pradesh and enjoy it's beauty!
